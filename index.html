So you think you can code.

Here in Portland we have a lot of great options for coding education. But first thing is first: which of the many coding languages is right for you?

Take this questionnaire to find your code soul-mate!

1. Are you focused on how things look or how they function?
2. Would you want to make something people interact with or something people depend on and dont even know it?
3. Would you prefer to be tucked away in a dark room building giant frameworks in your mind, then weaving them with your genius abilities, unfettered by co-workers; or in an interactive, busy, bright and open workspace?
4. What kind of auto shop sounds more interesting: a body shop fixing cosmetic problems and beautifying exterior elements, or a performance shop dialing in the effeciency of an engine?
5. Are you more artistic or analytical?
